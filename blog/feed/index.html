I have broken at the end of the last stage and was towed into the final time control (with Dave Turner in a Mitsubishi PIckup in 1995).  I was Matt Iorio's first-ever co-driver at the 2002 Lake Superior Rally.</p><p>Lake Superior is the site of my only ever national stage rally podium, in 2001 with Patrick Richard.  We finished third, behind Mark Lovell/Mike Kidd and Rhys Millen/Lauchlin O'Sullivan (yes, Lauchlin was actually co-driving for Rhys!).  It was a truly special weekend.</p><p>I love the scenery of the Upper Peninsula.  I love the stages and the unpredictability of the weather.  I love the friendly people, pasties at the Suomi Bakery, beers at the Library, fishbowls at the Ambassador...  I miss the old Library too (I did get to visit it before it burned).</p><p>Obviously I wish I was competing, but I look forward to bringing the excitement of the MaxAttack! two-wheel-drive rally series presented by the Danza del Sol winery to fans via the internet.  Look for updates on this page, of course, and also at <a href="http://www.specialstage.com">http://www.specialstage.com</a> and the occasional Tweet when there is breaking news.</p></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-taxonomy-vocabulary-3 field-type-taxonomy-term-reference field-label-above clearfix"><h3 class="field-label">Events: </h3><ul class="links"><li class="taxonomy-term-reference-0"><a href="/taxonomy/term/75">Lake Superior Performance Rally</a></li></ul></div> Wed, 13 Oct 2010 22:28:10 +0000 Ben 159 at http://www.max-attack.com http://www.max-attack.com/node/159#comments MAXATTACK!
